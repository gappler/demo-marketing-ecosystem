Voice

Core: Plainspoken · Confident · Restrained · Dry
▸ Assumes reader knows denier ratings, fill power, load ratings
▸ Use specs directly — no translation needed
Messaging
● Built for the job, not the brand
● Quality you verify, not quality we claim
○ Pay for the gear, not the logo
○ Responsible materials, zero grandstanding
Terminology
✓ built, engineered, rated, tested, durable
✗ revolutionary, game-changing, passionate
⚠ sustainable — state as fact, never lead with it
Style & Compliance
✗ No exclamation points
✗ No superlatives without evidence
✗ No lifestyle imagery or aspirational framing
✓ Minimal logo placement
✓ Eco claims as fact, not marketing
Copy Preview
"500D Cordura body with reinforced bottom panel. Framesheet and aluminum stays rated to 45 lbs. Rolltop closure. Four attachment points. 4 lbs 2 oz. This is not an ultralight pack. It's a pack that carries real weight over real terrain without falling apart."
Rules demonstrated: Technical register — specs stated directly, no translation. Confident — no hedging. Restrained — no superlatives. Honest about trade-offs (weight vs. durability).
